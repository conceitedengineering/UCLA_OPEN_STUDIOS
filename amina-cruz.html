I've been considering my relationship to the California landscape and my family’s history at the DiGiorgio Farm, as migrant workers. There are 2 main aspects to this work. Performance: Burying and uncovering power objects throughout various farms in Arvin, CA, where DiGiorgio was located. A cycle of energy between the earth and myself, creating a ritual. Photography/Archive: I enlarged family photos from the 1930s to 1970s which were taken at the DiGiorgio farm and encampment. I pasted the images in the areas where my family worked and lived. A reclaiming of land and filling in the archive.
