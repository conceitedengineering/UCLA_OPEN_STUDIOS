Bio: Saskia Baden (b. 1992) is a photographic artist from western North Carolina. She received  her BA in photography from Bard College in 2017. She works primarily in large-format  darkroom photography. Her practice is based in ideas of corporeality and mortality, which the  photographic medium simultaneously reveres and undermines.
