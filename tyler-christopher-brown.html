Tyler Christopher Brown is an interdisciplinary artist from Los Angeles, CA.

My sculptural practice examines the American institutional framework of economic inequality, class stratification, and political polarization under capitalism. Through the utilization of fragments and found objects, artworks strive to demystify systems of exploitation, oppression, and globalized imperialism.

He holds a BFA in Sculpture from CUNY Hunter College, New York. Currently, he is an MFA candidate at the University of California, Los Angeles.
